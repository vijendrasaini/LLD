# Standard LLD Problem Solving Approach

For every problem:

## Step 1

Gather Requirements

Ask:

* What features are needed?
* Any assumptions?
* Any constraints?

---

## Step 2

Identify Use Cases

Examples:

* User actions
* System actions

---

## Step 3

Identify Core Objects

Look for nouns in requirements.

Example:

Parking Lot

* Vehicle
* Slot
* Ticket
* Gate

---

## Step 4

Assign Responsibilities

Ask:

* Who owns this data?
* Who performs this action?
* Who should know about this information?

---

## Step 5

Draw UML

Focus on:

* Association
* Composition
* Aggregation
* Inheritance

---

## Step 6

Apply Design Patterns

Apply only when needed.

Never start with patterns.

---

## Step 7

Code

Implement core entities first.

---

## Step 8

Discuss Improvements

Examples:

* Scalability
* Extensibility
* Concurrency
* New Features

# Success Criteria

You should be able to solve a new LLD problem within:

* 10 minutes Requirements
* 10 minutes Use Cases
* 10 minutes UML
* 20 minutes Coding
* 10 minutes Discussion

Total: ~60 minutes
